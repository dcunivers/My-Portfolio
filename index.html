A little about me...

I'm a developer, model, and problem solver. I'm drawn to web development because it's like solving puzzles on a Friday night – irresistibly engaging and endlessly rewarding. I genuinely find joy in learning different coding languages and solving challenges head-on. Crafting memorable websites is a creative outlet where I can bring ideas to life.


Beyond the digital world, you'll find me in front of the camera, doing some sort of physical activities (mainly with my dog, Harley), having dinners with friends, or traveling the world. Development, for me, is not just about lines of code – it's about weaving a story through technology that reflects the diverse tapestry of my interests and experiences.
