Hi, I'm Dana.

A little about me...

I'm a front-end developer with a passion for fashion, specializing in crafting visually stunning and user-friendly websites.


I've always been curious about learning new languages (and cultures), seeing how things work, and creating with my own hands. My experience as a model has given me the freedom and drive in learning to code. Crafting memorable websites is another creative outlet that reflects the diverse tapestry of my interests and experiences.


When I'm not in between lines of code, you'll find me in front of the camera, doing some workout or physical activity (mainly with my dog, Harley), having cozy dinners with friends, or traveling the world. Development, for me, isn't just about lines of code – it's about weaving a story through technology where I can bring ideas to life. Let me help you create yours.

~ xo
